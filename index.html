P

Pull the Pin

Remove safety pin with right hand controller trigger

A

Aim at Base

Point nozzle at base of fire, not at flames

S

Squeeze Handle

Use left controller to activate extinguisher spray

S

Sweep Side to Side

Move nozzle across fire base systematically
